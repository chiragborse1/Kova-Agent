What it does
Built for real work
Not another chat wrapper. Kova is a full agent that runs tools, manages files, and remembers everything.
🧠

Persistent Memory

Remembers your projects, preferences, and context across every session. Pick up where you left off, every time.

🛠

Real Tool Use

Reads files, writes code, runs commands, browses the web, and manages your terminal. Not just text in, text out.

🎨

Learns Skills

Creates reusable skills from your workflows. The more you use it, the smarter it gets at helping you specifically.

💬

Multi-Platform

Works in your terminal, as a desktop app, or connected to Telegram, Discord, Slack, and 20+ messaging platforms.

🤖

Multi-Agent

Delegates complex tasks to sub-agents that work in parallel. Orchestrates across projects and tools automatically.

🔒

Runs Locally

Your data stays on your machine. No cloud dependency. Works with OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, or any OpenAI-compatible API.
